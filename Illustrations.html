Black and White Illustration Gallery

In this gallery, many of the works done are not actually pencil sketches! Rather, they are five minute works which are done based on an emotion. My inspirations for these works are based on artists such as Wu Guanzhong and Afandi. I have always had a fascination with Wu GuanZhong due to his abstract fauvism style whilst I've learnt to appreciate Afandi's perspective that the best artworks emotionally are those done within an hour.


Moving past these artworks, I shall next present my coloured illustrations in greater detail!
